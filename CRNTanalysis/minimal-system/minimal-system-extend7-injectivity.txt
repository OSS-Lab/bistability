                 MA INJECTIVITY REPORT: minimal-system-extend7
                 =============================================
 
     Taken with mass action kinetics, the network is injective in the sense of
 the references 2 and 4 below.
  
      In particular, there is no set of rate constants for which the resulting
 mass action system can admit two distinct strictly positive steady states that
 are stoichiometrically compatible or a degenerate steady state.
